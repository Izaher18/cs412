About Malcolm X

Malcolm X was an influential African American Muslim minister and human rights activist. He was a prominent figure during the civil rights movement and is known for his powerful speeches advocating for Black empowerment, self-defense, and racial justice. His autobiography, co-written with Alex Haley, remains one of the most influential works in American literature.
